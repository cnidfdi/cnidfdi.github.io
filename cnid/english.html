Chinese Investment Survey 2018


Dear Sir/Madam, We are conducting a survey on Chinese subsidiaries in Indonesia and hope to learn more about your company. The questionnaire is short and has been designed to minimize the time required to complete it: it will take approximately 5 minutes. The survey is conducted by Department of International Economics & Management, Copenhagen Business School (CBS), Denmark. The research project is purely scientific with no commercial application. All data collected will be treated confidentially, kept safe at CBS, and not relayed to any outside parties. We will be happy to provide you with a copy of the final research report at your request when it becomes available in 2019. The questionnaire primarily concerns organizational issues. The questions can be answered by most members of the management team. If the company has more than one office/factory in Indonesia, please report information about the entire subsidiary company, not just one office/factory. The purpose of the research is to establish an overview of Chinese investment in Indonesia and the challenges and opportunities faced by these subsidiaries. It is important to understand how investment flows can be better facilitated and the operating conditions for foreign subsidiaries better supported to the mutual benefit of China and Indonesia. Yours sincerely, Prof. Peter Gammeltoft, PhD
